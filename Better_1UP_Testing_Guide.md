# Better 1UP
### Official Testing Guide

A step-by-step 60-minute testing flow for 1 Teacher and 11 Students.

---

# 👩‍🏫 1. The Teacher Role (1 Person)
**Goal:** Setup the environment and invite the students.

1. **Sign Up:** Open the app and create your basic profile (just a username).
2. **Elevate to Teacher:** Go to the **Account** tab, select "Teacher", and enter your email address. You will receive a secure 6-digit verification code. Enter it to permanently unlock teacher features.
3. **Create the Classroom:** Navigate to **Classes**, tap "Create Class" and name it (e.g., *Alpha Testers*). 
4. **Invite the Group:** A 6-character "Join Code" will be generated. You can also project the live QR code on your screen for the 11 students to scan.

---

# 🙋‍♂️ 2. The Student Role (11 People)
**Goal:** Join the platform, link up, and configure personal profiles.

1. **Join the Class:** Open the app. There are no passwords or emails required. Enter your desired username. 
2. **Connect to Teacher:** Go to **Classes → Join Class** and enter the 6-character code (or scan the QR code). You will instantly appear in the Teacher's live dashboard.
3. **Connect as Friends:** Go to the **Friends** tab. Tap "Create Invite Link" and swap codes with another tester to become friends. You will now see their points on your private leaderboard.
4. **Setup Push Reminders:** Go to **Account → App Settings → Push Reminders** and set up a daily training reminder.

---

# 🚀 3. The Live Class Test
**Goal:** Experience the real-time synchronous classroom network.

1. **The Diagnostic Run:** Once all 11 students are actively visible as "Online" in the Classroom view, the Teacher clicks **Start Sprint** and chooses the **Diagnostic Sprint**.
2. **The Countdown:** All 11 student devices will instantly lock into the sprint screen. You have 60 seconds to answer as many problems as possible!
3. **Review the Heatmap:** When time is up, the Teacher will see a combined 10x10 *Classroom Mastery Grid* showing where the collective cohort struggled most.

---

# 📈 4. Individual Progression
**Goal:** Independent learning, analytics, and unlocking the 20x20 bounds.

- **Check Your Analytics:** Go to the **Progress** tab. Review your personal Mastery Grid to see your exact "Weakest Concept" and "Strongest Skill".
- **Train Independently:** Start a **Training Sprint** directly from the Lobby. The adaptive engine will now *specifically* target the weaknesses it mapped out during the Diagnostic Run.
- **Climb the Leaderboards:** Check your friends and classroom leaderboards to see how your weighted points compare.
- **Reach the 20x20 Domain:** Continue answering quickly and accurately (< 2 seconds). Hitting 80% accuracy will dynamically unlock higher tiers, progressively expanding your multiplication limit up to **20x20**!

---
